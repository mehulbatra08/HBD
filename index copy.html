H.B.D. PHARMA

Empowering Healthcare Since 1987

Experience. Reach. Reliability.

Established in 1987, H.B.D. Pharma has emerged as one of India's most trusted pharmaceutical wholesalers, operating from Delhi, Bhagirath Palace — Asia's largest medicine market.

With over three decades of proven reliability, we have grown into a nationally recognised name, supplying a comprehensive range of healthcare solutions — from specialized oncology care and other super specialty drugs, to essential ethical and generic medicines, ayurvedic healthcare, surgical supplies, hospital essentials, and hard-to-find pharmaceuticals.

More Than Medicine — A Commitment to Care
