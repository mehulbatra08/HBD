About H.B.D. Pharma

Established in 1987, H.B.D. Pharma has grown from a small family-run venture in Delhi to one of India's most trusted pharmaceutical wholesalers. For over three decades, we have stood at the intersection of healthcare access, quality supply, and social responsibility, working tirelessly to support the well-being of individuals, communities, and healthcare systems across the country. From urban hospitals to remote clinics, from pharmacy chains to individual healthcare providers, we ensure that life-saving products reach where they're needed, when they're needed.

Through efficient logistics, ethical sourcing, and personalized service, we aim to reduce gaps in the healthcare chain. Every product we deliver carries our commitment to make healthcare more accessible, consistent, and impactful.


We feel honored and humbled to be able to support the health of our society, especially in critical times. Being a helping hand in someone's healing journey is not just our duty, it's a privilege we deeply respect.

Our Journey Through Time

1987

Foundation & Early Beginnings

H.B.D. Pharma was established in Delhi's Bhagirath Palace, Asia's largest medicine market. Started as a small pharmaceutical wholesaler with a vision to serve the healthcare community with integrity and reliability.

1990s

Expansion & Diversification

Expanded our product range to include surgical supplies, hospital essentials, and medical equipment. Built strong relationships with leading pharmaceutical manufacturers and established our reputation for quality and timely delivery.

2000s

Specialized Healthcare Solutions

Introduced specialized oncology medicines and super-specialty drugs. Became a trusted partner for hospitals and clinics requiring critical and hard-to-find medications, particularly in cancer treatment and rare disease management.

2010s

Nationwide Network

Established a comprehensive nationwide delivery network, enabling us to serve clients from metros to remote towns across India. Implemented advanced logistics and inventory management systems for efficient operations.

Present

Industry Leadership

Today, H.B.D. Pharma stands as a leading pharmaceutical wholesaler with 38+ years of excellence. We continue to innovate and adapt, maintaining our commitment to healthcare accessibility while embracing modern distribution technologies.
