Products & Services

Delivering Excellence in Healthcare Solutions

At H.B.D. Pharma, we recognize that healthcare solutions must be as diverse and dynamic as the patients they serve — and so is our supply portfolio. With over 38 years of industry experience, we have built lasting partnerships with pharmacies, hospitals, clinics, and wellness providers across India. Our commitment lies in sourcing only high-quality, fully compliant products from licensed manufacturers and ensuring their timely and reliable distribution. We take pride in being a trusted link in the healthcare supply chain — delivering precision, consistency, and peace of mind to every client.

Prescription & Generic Medicines

We procure a wide range of ethical and generic medicines that are trusted by healthcare professionals across India. Each product is sourced from licensed, reputable manufacturers and meets stringent quality and regulatory standards.

Whether supporting day-to-day treatments or managing chronic conditions, we help ensure timely access to safe, effective, and affordable medicines — delivered with the consistency and care our clients rely on.

Oncology & Super-Specialty Medicines

Cancer care demands precision, urgency, and compassion — and we approach it with the seriousness it deserves. At H.B.D. Pharma, we procure and distribute a critical range of oncology and super-specialty medicines, including targeted therapies, life-saving injectables, and essential support drugs.

Our processes ensure secure sourcing, proper handling, and robust cold-chain logistics — minimizing delays and safeguarding product integrity. We work closely with healthcare providers to help ensure that patients receive the treatments they need, when they need them most.

Ayurvedic and Homeopathic Remedies

Rooted in India's rich medical heritage, ayurvedic and homeopathic treatments continue to play an important role in modern healthcare. At H.B.D. Pharma, we offer a thoughtfully selected range of certified remedies that blend time-tested traditions with today's quality and safety standards.

Each product is ensured with purity, efficacy, and consistency — supporting holistic treatment plans with natural, patient-focused options.

Surgical & Hospital Supplies

We support healthcare professionals in maintaining safe, sterile, and well-equipped clinical environments by offering a reliable selection of surgical and hospital essentials. Our catalogue includes disposables, IV sets, gloves, wound care products, PPE, and other critical supplies.

All items are sourced from verified manufacturers and delivered with speed and accuracy, helping our partners focus on patient care without supply interruptions.

Medical Electronic Components

Modern healthcare relies on precision — and the technology behind it. We provide essential electronic components and devices that support diagnostics, monitoring, and treatment systems in clinical settings.

From ECG parts and nebulizer units to sensors and monitoring equipment, each item is tested for performance and reliability. Our goal is to help healthcare facilities stay equipped with the tools they need to deliver accurate, timely care.

Medical Cosmetics & Therapeutic Skincare

Post-treatment care and dermatological health require products that are both effective and safe. At H.B.D. Pharma, we offer a curated selection of medicated cosmetics and therapeutic skincare solutions — trusted by dermatologists, clinics, and wellness professionals.

From formulations that support skin recovery to treatments used in cosmetology and chronic skin conditions, each product is selected for its clinical efficacy, safety profile, and regulatory compliance.

FMCG & OTC Products

Everyday health and wellness rely on accessible, over-the-counter solutions. We offer a dependable selection of fast-moving consumer healthcare goods and OTC essentials — including hygiene products, pain relief, antiseptics, wellness supplements, and more.

Our goal is to help pharmacies, retailers, and clinics meet daily public demand with quality-assured products that are ready when needed — backed by efficient distribution and trusted sourcing.

Supplements & Nutraceuticals

Preventive care and long-term wellness often begin with the right nutritional support. At H.B.D. Pharma, we offer a well-researched range of supplements and nutraceuticals — including essential vitamins, immune boosters, protein formulations, and condition-specific blends.

Sourced from certified manufacturers and trusted by healthcare professionals, our products are designed to complement treatment plans, promote recovery, and support overall well-being.

Vaccines

Vaccination is one of the most vital pillars of public health — and we treat its distribution with the care it demands. H.B.D. Pharma supplies a broad range of routine and specialty vaccines, including pediatric, adult, and travel-related immunizations.

With stringent cold-chain protocols, verified sourcing, and precise handling, we ensure vaccine integrity from manufacturer warehouse to point of care — supporting timely immunization and community protection across all age groups.

Our Commitment

At H.B.D. Pharma, we do more than deliver healthcare products — we uphold a responsibility to those who care for others. Every item we procure and distribute reflects our dedication to quality, reliability, and long-term partnership.

Our clients trust us not just for what we provide, but for how we operate: with integrity, responsiveness, and a deep understanding of the critical role we play in the healthcare ecosystem. As needs evolve, we remain committed to growing with our partners — consistently, carefully, and with purpose.
